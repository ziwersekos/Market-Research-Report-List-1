<p><strong>The Vapour Recovery Units (VRU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-type Recovery</li><li>Closed-type Recovery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapour Recovery Units (VRUs) are systems designed to capture and condense volatile organic compounds emitted during the transfer of liquids, primarily in the oil and gas industry. The market is divided into open-type and closed-type recovery systems. Open-type recovery allows gas to be vented to the atmosphere, capturing vapours at a lower efficiency. In contrast, closed-type recovery systems capture and store these vapours for reuse or disposal, providing higher efficiency and environmental compliance, thus driving their adoption in stricter regulatory environments.</p></p>
