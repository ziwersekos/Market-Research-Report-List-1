<p><p>The Three-Phase Current Relays market is primarily segmented into under-current detection and over-current detection types. Conversely, over-current detection relays safeguard systems by triggering alarms or disconnects when current levels exceed predefined limits, preventing damage to electrical components. Together, these relay types enhance system reliability and efficiency across various industries by providing essential protection mechanisms against current-related issues.</p></p>
