<p><p>The Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems market includes specialized types such as Trench Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems and Flange Fire Fighting Pre-action Systems. Trench systems are designed for installations in narrow spaces, usually protecting underground utilities or structures from fire, while Flange systems connect to piping flanges for efficient fire suppression. Both systems use a two-step activation process to reduce accidental discharge, combining the advantages of wet and dry systems, thereby enhancing safety in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
