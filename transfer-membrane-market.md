Nitrocellulose membranes, known for their superior protein transfer efficiency, are commonly used in Western blotting. Nylon membranes offer excellent mechanical strength and versatility, suitable for a range of laboratory processes. Each type addresses specific needs in protein transfer, ensuring optimal results in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
