<p><p>The turbo molecular pumps market comprises various types, including magnetically suspended and oil-lubricated pumps. Magnetically suspended pumps utilize magnetic bearings to support the rotor, minimizing friction and wear, which enhances efficiency and longevity. Oil-lubricated pumps use a film of oil to reduce friction between moving parts, offering robust performance in demanding applications but requiring maintenance for oil management. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized pumps for niche applications, catering to diverse sectors like semiconductor manufacturing and research laboratories.</p></p>
