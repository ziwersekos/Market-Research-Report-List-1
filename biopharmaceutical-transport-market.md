<p><strong>The Biopharmaceutical Transport Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Transport</li><li>Non-cold Chain Transport</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopharmaceutical transport market comprises two primary categories: cold chain transport and non-cold chain transport. Cold chain transport involves temperature-controlled logistics to preserve the integrity of temperature-sensitive biopharmaceuticals, such as vaccines and biologics, ensuring they remain effective throughout the supply chain. Non-cold chain transport deals with products that are stable at ambient temperatures, allowing for more flexible and less stringent logistics. Both types are essential in ensuring the safe delivery of biopharmaceuticals to healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
