<p><strong>The Vehicle Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent Suspension</li><li>Non-independent Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle suspension system market is primarily divided into two types: independent and non-independent suspension. Independent suspension systems allow each wheel to move independently, enhancing ride comfort and handling, making them popular in passenger vehicles. Non-independent suspension systems link the wheels, ensuring that movement in one affects the other, which can be more durable and cost-effective, often found in trucks and off-road vehicles. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying consumer needs and driving conditions.</p></p>
