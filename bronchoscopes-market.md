<p><ul><li>Rigid Bronchoscopes</li><li>Flexible Bronchoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bronchoscope market is segmented into two primary types: rigid and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are sturdy, straight tubes used primarily for therapeutic procedures, allowing direct visualization of the airway and enabling interventions like biopsy or dilation. In contrast, flexible bronchoscopes are slender, more maneuverable instruments designed for diagnostic purposes, reaching deeper into the respiratory tract for visualization and sampling. Both types serve critical roles in respiratory medicine, catering to different clinical needs and applications.</p></p>
