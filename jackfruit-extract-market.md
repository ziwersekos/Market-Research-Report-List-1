<p><p>The jackfruit extract market is categorized based on extraction ratios, primarily 1:10, 1:20, and 1:30. A 1:10 extraction means one part of jackfruit is used to extract ten parts of solvent, yielding a concentrated product rich in nutrients. The 1:20 extraction is less concentrated, utilizing one part of jackfruit for twenty parts of solvent, providing a balance between potency and gentleness. The 1:30 extraction is the least concentrated, catering to markets seeking milder extracts, often for dietary supplements or flavoring applications.</p></p>
