<p><p>The GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) rebars market comprises two primary types: smooth GFRP rebars and sand-coated GFRP rebars. Smooth GFRP rebars feature a uniform, non-textured surface, offering ease of installation and effective bonding with concrete. In contrast, sand-coated GFRP rebars have an added textured finish, enhancing grip and improving adhesion with the surrounding concrete, which can increase load-bearing capacity. Both types provide excellent corrosion resistance and durability, making them suitable for various construction applications.</p></p>
