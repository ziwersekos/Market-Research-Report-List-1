Low suction fans are suitable for homes with minimal radon levels and provide basic ventilation. Moderate suction fans are designed for average radon concentrations and effectively reduce radon gas accumulation. High suction fans are used for buildings with severe radon issues, offering robust performance to ensure significant gas removal and ensure a safe indoor environment.</p></p>
