<p><p>The hospital disinfection robots market is primarily categorized into UV-C and HPV systems. UV-C robots utilize ultraviolet light to effectively neutralize pathogens on surfaces and in the air, providing rapid disinfection. HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) robots, on the other hand, release hydrogen peroxide vapor to decontaminate entire rooms, ensuring comprehensive coverage and eliminating various microorganisms. Both technologies enhance infection control in healthcare settings, promoting safer environments for patients and staff while reducing the risk of hospital-acquired infections.</p></p>
