<p><p>The smart irrigation sprinkler controllers market is divided into two main types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data and forecasts to optimize watering schedules, ensuring efficient water use while adapting to climatic changes. In contrast, sensor-based controllers rely on soil moisture sensors and other environmental inputs to determine the exact watering needs of plants, preventing over- or under-watering. Both types enhance water conservation while maintaining healthy landscapes, contributing to sustainable irrigation practices.</p></p>
