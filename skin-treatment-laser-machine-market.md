<p><p>The skin treatment laser machine market comprises two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layer of skin, promoting regeneration and addressing conditions like wrinkles and scars. They provide more dramatic results but require longer recovery times. Non-ablative lasers, on the other hand, penetrate the skin without damaging the surface, stimulating collagen production to improve skin texture and tone with minimal downtime. Both types are popular for various skin rejuvenation and treatment applications.</p></p>
