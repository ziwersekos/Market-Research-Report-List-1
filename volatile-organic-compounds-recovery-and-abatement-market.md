<p><p>The Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) Recovery and Abatement Market comprises various technologies for managing VOC emissions. Regenerative Thermal Oxidation (RTO) captures and oxidizes VOCs to minimize emissions. Recuperative Thermal Oxidation uses heat exchange to improve energy efficiency. Catalytic Oxidation employs catalysts to enhance oxidation processes at lower temperatures. Photo-oxidation utilizes UV light to break down VOCs. Adsorption by Activated Carbon captures VOCs on carbon surfaces, while Rotor Concentrators concentrate emissions for further treatment. Cryocondensation involves cooling VOCs to condense and recover them. Each method offers distinct advantages for controlling VOC pollution.</p></p>
