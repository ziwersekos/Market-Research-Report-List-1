<p><p>The optical position sensors market is categorized into three main types. One-dimensional optical position sensors measure position along a single axis, primarily used in applications requiring basic linear movement detection. Two-dimensional optical position sensors capture position data in both horizontal and vertical planes, ideal for touchscreens and automated systems. Multi-axial optical position sensors offer comprehensive tracking across multiple axes, suitable for complex robotic and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs in precision measurement and control.</p></p>
