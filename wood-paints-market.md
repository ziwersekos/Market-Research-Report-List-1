<p><p>The wood paints market is primarily divided into waterborne and oil-based types. Waterborne paints are environmentally friendly, offering quick drying times, low VOC emissions, and easy cleanup with water. They provide excellent color retention and durability, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Oil-based paints, on the other hand, are known for their richness and durability, creating a strong finish that withstands wear and weather. However, they typically have longer drying times and higher VOC levels, requiring careful handling.</p></p>
