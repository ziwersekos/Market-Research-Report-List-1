<p><p>Ultrasonic fatigue testing machines are categorized primarily into three types. The Push-Pull type applies axial loads to test materials' fatigue strength through linear oscillations, ideal for tensile and compressive studies. The Rotating Bending type subjects materials to cyclic bending stresses via a rotating shaft, simulating real-world applications like shafts and beams. Other types may include combined testing setups or specific configurations tailored for unique material properties or testing conditions, enhancing versatility in fatigue analysis across various industries.</p></p>
