<p><p>The micronutrients market can be categorized into three main types: powder, granule, and liquid. Powdered micronutrients are highly concentrated and easily mixed into various formulations, making them versatile for application. Granules offer a slow-release mechanism, allowing for prolonged nutrient availability in soil, which is ideal for agricultural use. Liquid micronutrients are often favored for foliar applications due to their quick absorption by plants, leading to rapid nutrient uptake. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs and delivery methods for optimal crop growth.</p></p>
