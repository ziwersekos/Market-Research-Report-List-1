<p><ul><li>Mechanical Stamping Briquette Press</li><li>Screw Briquetting Machine</li><li>Hydraulic Briquette Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biomass briquette machine market consists of three main types: Mechanical Stamping Briquette Press, Screw Briquetting Machine, and Hydraulic Briquette Machine. Mechanical stamping briquette presses use mechanical force to compress biomass into solid briquettes and are known for high production efficiency. Screw briquetting machines employ a screw mechanism for continuous briquette formation, ideal for various feedstocks. Hydraulic briquette machines utilize hydraulic pressure for densifying biomass, offering high density and strong briquette quality, catering to diverse operational needs in biomass processing.</p></p>
