<p><strong>The Rapid Diagnostic Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow Test</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow Through</li><li>Solid Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Diagnostic Kits market is categorized into four primary types: </p><p>1. **Lateral Flow Tests** - Simple devices that deliver results visually, often used for home testing.</p><p>2. **Agglutination Assays** - Tests that detect antigens or antibodies through the clumping of particles, useful in blood typing and infectious diseases.</p><p>3. **Flow Through Tests** - Similar to lateral flow but utilize a different mechanism, allowing fluids to move through a membrane for diagnosis.</p><p>4. **Solid Phase Tests** - Involve immobilized reagents on a solid substrate, enhancing sensitivity for complex analyses.</p></p>
