<p><p>Oil burner nozzles are crucial components in combustion systems, primarily categorized into solid and hollow types. Solid nozzles deliver a concentrated stream of fuel, ideal for high-velocity applications, providing efficient and effective combustion. In contrast, hollow nozzles disperse fuel in a fine mist, which enhances atomization and allows for better mixing with air, improving combustion efficiency and reducing emissions. Both types serve specific applications in heating and industrial processes, catering to varying operational needs.</p></p>
