<p><ul><li>Liquid Type</li><li>Granular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen slow-release fertilizer market consists of two primary types: liquid and granular. Liquid slow-release fertilizers are typically water-soluble and can be easily applied through irrigation systems, providing a quick nutrient boost while gradually releasing nitrogen over time. Granular slow-release fertilizers, on the other hand, are solid pellets that release nitrogen at a controlled rate, often through microbial activity or soil moisture. Both types cater to different application needs, enhancing nutrient availability for crops while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
