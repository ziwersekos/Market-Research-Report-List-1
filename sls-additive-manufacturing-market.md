<p><strong>The SLS Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting(SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SLS Additive Manufacturing market encompasses technologies like Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high precision. EBM, on the other hand, employs a focused electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, enabling faster build times and the production of large components. Both technologies are integral to industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical, offering innovative solutions for rapid prototyping and production.</p></p>
