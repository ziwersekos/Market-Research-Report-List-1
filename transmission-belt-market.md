<p><p>The transmission belt market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Flat belts, known for their simplicity, are commonly used in low-power applications. V belts, designed with a trapezoidal shape, offer better grip and are ideal for high-torque scenarios. Gear belts, featuring teeth for precise synchronization, are utilized in timing applications and machinery requiring exact rotation. Additionally, the "other" category includes specialized belts like round belts and synchronous belts, catering to unique industrial needs. Together, they serve diverse operational demands across multiple sectors.</p></p>
