<p><strong>The Oral Antidiabetic Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfonylureas</li><li>Meglitinides</li><li>Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral antidiabetic drugs market encompasses various classes, including sulfonylureas, meglitinides, and alpha-glucosidase inhibitors. Sulfonylureas stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas, effectively lowering blood glucose levels in type 2 diabetes patients. Meglitinides also promote insulin release but act more quickly and for shorter durations, making them suitable for post-meal glucose control. Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, helping manage blood sugar spikes after meals. Together, these drug types cater to diverse patient needs in diabetes management.</p></p>
