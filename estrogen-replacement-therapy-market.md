<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Estrogen Replacement Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oral</li><li>Topical</li><li>Transdermal</li><li>Vaginal</li><li>Parenteral</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Estrogen Replacement Therapy (ERT) encompasses several market types based on administration routes. Oral forms include tablets and capsules, offering convenience but varying absorption. Topical options include creams and gels applied to the skin, providing localized effects. Transdermal patches deliver a steady dose of estrogen through the skin for sustained release. Vaginal preparations, like creams or rings, target localized symptoms effectively. Parenteral forms involve injections, ensuring direct bloodstream entry for rapid effects, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
