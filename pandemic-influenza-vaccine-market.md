<p><strong>The Pandemic Influenza Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Virus Vaccines</li><li>Split Virus Vaccines</li><li>Subunit Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pandemic Influenza Vaccine market includes three main types: Whole Virus Vaccines, Split Virus Vaccines, and Subunit Vaccines. Whole Virus Vaccines utilize the entire inactivated virus, providing a broad immune response. Split Virus Vaccines contain virus particles that are disrupted, retaining immunogenic properties while minimizing side effects. Subunit Vaccines are composed of specific viral proteins, targeting the immune response more precisely. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of safety, efficacy, and production scalability during pandemic situations.</p></p>
