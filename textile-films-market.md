<p><p>The textile films market comprises three primary types: polyethylene (PE), polypropylene (PP), and polyurethane (PU). PE films are widely used for their lightweight and moisture-resistant properties, making them ideal for applications in packaging and agricultural products. PP films offer higher tensile strength and resistance to chemicals, suitable for various industrial uses. PU films are known for their flexibility and durability, often utilized in specialized applications like medical textiles and protective clothing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
