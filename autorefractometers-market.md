<p><p>The autorefractometer market is segmented into two main types: automatic keratometers and manual keratometers. Automatic keratometers utilize advanced technology to measure corneal curvature and provide precise refractive data quickly and efficiently, making them ideal for busy clinical settings. In contrast, manual keratometers require the operator to perform measurements manually, allowing for greater control and flexibility but often taking more time. Both types serve essential roles in assessing vision and guiding refractive surgery or corrective lens prescriptions.</p></p>
