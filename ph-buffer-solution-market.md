<p><ul><li>Acidic Buffer Solutions</li><li>Alkaline Buffer Solutions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pH buffer solution market is categorized into two main types: acidic buffer solutions and alkaline buffer solutions. Acidic buffers maintain a stable pH in the range below 7, using weak acids and their conjugate bases to resist changes when acids or bases are added. Alkaline buffers, on the other hand, maintain a pH above 7, utilizing weak bases and their conjugate acids. These solutions are essential in various applications, including pharmaceuticals, biological research, and industrial processes, ensuring optimal conditions for reactions.</p></p>
