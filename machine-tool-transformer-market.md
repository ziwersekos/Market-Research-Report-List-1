<p><ul><li>Single-phase Machine Tool Transformer</li><li>Three-phase Machine Tool Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool transformer market is divided into two main types: single-phase and three-phase transformers. Single-phase machine tool transformers are typically used for smaller applications, providing power to individual machines and delivering lower voltage levels. In contrast, three-phase machine tool transformers are designed for larger operations, efficiently supplying power to multiple machines simultaneously. They offer higher voltage output, improved energy efficiency, and are suitable for complex industrial setups requiring substantial power for heavy-duty machining tasks.</p></p>
