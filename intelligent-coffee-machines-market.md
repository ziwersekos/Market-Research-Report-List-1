<p><p>Intelligent coffee machines can be classified into WiFi-enabled and Bluetooth-enabled categories. WiFi-enabled machines connect to the internet, allowing users to operate and monitor them remotely through dedicated apps. This feature facilitates advanced functionalities such as scheduling and integrating with smart home systems. In contrast, Bluetooth-enabled coffee machines offer direct device-to-device connectivity, enabling users to control brewing settings via smartphones or tablets within a limited range. Both types enhance convenience and customization for the coffee brewing experience.</p></p>
