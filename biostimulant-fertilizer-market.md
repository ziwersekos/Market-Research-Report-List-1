<p><p>The biostimulant fertilizer market is primarily categorized into two types: acid-based biostimulant fertilizers and plant extract biostimulant fertilizers. Acid-based biostimulants typically contain organic acids that enhance nutrient uptake and promote plant growth through improved root development and soil health. In contrast, plant extract biostimulants are derived from natural sources like seaweed, herbal extracts, or microbial metabolites, which stimulate plant growth by enhancing stress tolerance and promoting beneficial microbiome interactions in the soil, thereby improving overall crop productivity.</p></p>
