<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Disk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unimorph</li><li>Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric disk market is primarily segmented into unimorph and bimorph types. Unimorph devices consist of a single piezoelectric layer bonded to a substrate, generating movement when voltage is applied. They are typically used in applications requiring compact designs. Bimorph systems, on the other hand, consist of two bonded layers of piezoelectric material, allowing for greater displacement and force generation. This makes bimorphs suitable for applications like actuators and sensors where enhanced performance is essential. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive and electronics.</p></p>
