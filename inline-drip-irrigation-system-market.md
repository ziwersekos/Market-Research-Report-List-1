<p><strong>The Inline Drip Irrigation System Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface</li><li>Subsurface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline drip irrigation system market encompasses two main applications: surface and subsurface irrigation. Surface drip systems distribute water directly onto the soil surface, ensuring efficient moisture delivery to crops while minimizing evaporation and runoff. In contrast, subsurface drip irrigation involves placing the drip lines below the soil surface, promoting deep root development and further reducing water loss. Both applications enhance agricultural productivity by optimizing water use, making them essential in regions facing water scarcity and aiming for sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
