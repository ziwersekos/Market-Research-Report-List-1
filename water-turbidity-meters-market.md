<p><p>The water turbidity meters market is categorized into three primary types: desktop meters, portable meters, and inline meters. Desktop meters are stationary devices used in laboratories for precise analysis of water clarity. Portable meters are lightweight and designed for field use, allowing for quick measurements in various locations. Inline meters are installed directly in water systems, providing continuous turbidity monitoring in real-time, which is essential for process control in industrial and municipal applications. Each type serves unique operational needs and settings.</p></p>
