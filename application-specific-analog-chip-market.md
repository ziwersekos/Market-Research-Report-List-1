<p><p>The application-specific analog chip market is divided primarily into two categories: analog signal integrated circuits (ICs) and mixed-signal ICs. Analog signal ICs process continuous signals, focusing on applications like amplifiers, voltage regulators, and sensors. Mixed-signal ICs, on the other hand, handle both analog and digital signals, enabling functionalities such as data conversion and signal processing in more complex systems. These types cater to diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications, driving innovation and efficiency in electronic designs.</p></p>
