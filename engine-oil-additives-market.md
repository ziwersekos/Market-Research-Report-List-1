<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Additive Package</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine oil additives market is categorized into two main types: single component additives and additive packages. Single component additives are specific chemicals added to enhance particular properties of engine oil, such as viscosity or wear protection. In contrast, additive packages are pre-blended formulations containing multiple additives designed to improve overall performance, stability, and efficiency of engine oils. These packages simplify the blending process for manufacturers while ensuring a balanced and effective product for various engine types.</p></p>
