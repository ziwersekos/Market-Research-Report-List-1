<p><p>The travel software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility and scalable access for users from any location with internet connectivity. This model typically involves subscription fees and ensures automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a user's hardware, providing greater control and security but requiring significant initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to diverse business needs and operational preferences in the travel industry.</p></p>
